About the Studio

Gigi's Atelier started with a simple observation: most small and growing brands are either underserved by design — stuck with templates that don't hold up — or overserved by agencies built for budgets they don't have. We built a studio in between: senior-level thinking, delivered with the care of a small team that treats every project as its own.

Mission

To transform ideas into meaningful visual experiences that help businesses grow — combining creativity, strategy, and craft in equal measure.

Vision

A world where independent brands compete on the strength of their ideas, not the size of their design budget.

Philosophy

Design is more than aesthetics. It's a functional tool for communication, trust, and differentiation — every choice should earn its place.

Design Process

A seven-stage process — from discovery through final delivery — built to keep strategy and craft aligned at every step. See the full process below.

+Senior design attention on every project — no hand-offs to junior teams.
+Strategy before style: every direction is grounded in your audience and market.
+Production-ready deliverables — print-ready files, specs, and guidelines included.
+A collaborative process with clear milestones, not a black box.
+Experience across packaging, print, and digital — one studio, full consistency.
+Honest counsel — we'll tell you when an idea doesn't serve the brand.
